Case Study Of Moizik

Overview: The Financial Dashboard of Moizik provides a comprehensive analysis of the company’s financial performance, key business metrics, and customer trends. Designed for real-time monitoring, the dashboard offers valuable insights into sales, profit, expenses, and customer satisfaction, aiding decision-makers in optimizing strategies and ensuring sustained growth.

Objectives: 1.Track Financial Performance – Monitor revenue, profit margins, and EBITDA. 2.Analyze Customer Acquisition – Identify new, returning, and regular customers. 3.Optimize Product Sales – Evaluate sales trends for different product categories. 4.Control Expenses – Breakdown and manage various expenditure categories. 5.Measure Customer Satisfaction – Maintain high customer satisfaction levels.

Key Features: Monthly Sales & Gross Profit:The dashboard highlights a total monthly sales figure of 67,800, exceeding the goal of 25,000 (+171.2%).Gross profit stands at 27,800, significantly surpassing the goal of 4,000 (+595%). Profitability Metrics:The Gross Profit Margin is at 49.77%, exceeding the target of 20% (+148.86%).EBITDA (Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization) is 27,257, well above the goal of 3,000 (+808.57%). Customer Satisfaction Rate: Moizik maintains a 93.00% customer satisfaction rate, surpassing the 90.00% target (+3.33%). EBITDA and Profitability:The EBITDA and Gross Profit comparison visually represents the company’s financial stability, showing a significant contribution of profit to EBITDA. Revenue and Gross Profit Analysis:Monthly revenue and gross profit are analyzed, with peak performance observed in early months. Trends highlight seasonal variations and opportunities for revenue maximization.Customer Acquisition: The dashboard differentiates between new, returning, and regular customers.The graph indicates fluctuations in customer retention and acquisition over time.Sales of Each Product: Products are categorized into Clothing, Furniture, and Electronics.Sales trends reveal peak performance in certain months, guiding inventory and marketing strategies. Expense Breakdown:Expenses are divided into Admin, Marketing, Domain, Operational Expenditures (opEX), and other heads.The analysis helps in optimizing cost control and improving profitability. Sales Amount vs Target Sales:The company consistently meets and exceeds target sales, reflecting strong market performance. Discount vs Profit:The pie chart demonstrates that 98.31% of revenue is profit, while only 1.69% is attributed to discounts.This indicates an effective pricing strategy with minimal reliance on discounts to drive sales. Product Category Distribution:Sales are evenly distributed among Clothing, Electronics, and Furniture, each contributing 33.33%.This balanced distribution prevents over-dependence on a single product category. Insights & Recommendations: Revenue Growth: Strengthen marketing efforts during months with low revenue. Customer Retention: Implement loyalty programs to increase the percentage of returning customers. Expense Management: Optimize administrative and operational expenses to improve profitability. Sales Strategy: Use seasonal trends to enhance promotional campaigns for peak months. Satisfaction Improvement: Continue monitoring customer satisfaction rates to maintain high standards. Conclusion:The Financial Dashboard of Moizik serves as a powerful tool for financial analysis and strategic decision-making. By leveraging real-time insights, the company can enhance profitability, optimize operations, and ensure sustainable growth. The well-structured visual representation enables efficient data interpretation, fostering data-driven business success.
